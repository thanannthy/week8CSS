The Origin of Tea

Based on Ancient China Discovery

The origin of tea can be traced back to ancient China. According to legend, tea was discovered by Emperor Shen Nong, who ruled China around 2737 BCE. While he was boiling water one day, a few tea leaves accidentally fell into his pot. The emperor found the resulting beverage to be refreshing and enjoyed its taste and aroma.
